about

Shuhan Li is a singer-songwriter currently experimenting in sound. Classically trained with over a decade of piano and violin, she now writes songs around her own experiences and made-up stories.

Her debut singles “moon”, “risk”, and “train station” are out now.

songwriting has always been my favorite way to express myself and process what i’m going through. simultaneously, it’s been a unique opportunity for me to explore different perspectives and create stories surrounding bits and pieces of my everyday life.

inspired by The 1975, Olivia Rodrigo, and a collection of other artists and songs that i listen to frequently, intentionality is the most important element of each thing i write. although i’m still at the beginning of my journey in music, i'm excited for what’s to come.

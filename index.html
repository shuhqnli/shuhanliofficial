about

Shuhan Li is a singer-songwriter crafting emotionally intentional pop music that pulls from indie's warmth and R&B's introspection. Her songs explore the full landscape of love: the yearning that keeps you up at night, the heartbreak that reshapes you, and the terrifying beauty of risking it all for connection.

In music longer than she hasn't, Shuhan's classically trained background in piano and violin has shaped her journey, one that's led to international wins in music and songwriting competitions, including PLUETA.

With a visual world built on burgundy depths and metallic gold accents, streetwear confidence and romantic elegance, Shuhan is carving out her own lane. Every lyric is placed with purpose. This is just the beginning, and you're invited to watch it unfold.
